Helping investors and financial institutions make smarter decisions by cutting through the complexity of Belgian broker fee structures — in real time, with AI precision.

🎯 The Problem We Solve

Belgian retail investors and financial advisors face a fragmented, opaque landscape of broker fees. Key pain points include:

  • Fee structures are buried in dense PDFs, multiple web pages, and legalese — impossible to compare at a glance
  • Hidden costs (FX markups, custody fees, connectivity charges) are systematically underestimated
  • No single source of truth exists across Bolero, Keytrade, Degiro, ING Self Invest, Rebel, Revolut, and Trade Republic
  • Sales and advisory teams waste hours manually researching and updating fee tables
  • Investors make suboptimal decisions due to lack of personalised, scenario-based cost analysis

🤖 Our Solution

BE-Invest is an end-to-end AI platform that automatically extracts, validates, and serves up-to-date broker fee intelligence:

  • AI Extraction Pipeline — GPT-4o scrapes and parses broker PDFs & web pages automatically
  • Deterministic Fee Engine — Pure-Python rule engine ensures 100 % accurate cost calculations
  • Natural Language Chat — Users ask "Which broker is cheapest for ETF trading at €5,000?" and get instant answers
  • Investor Persona Profiles — Tailored TCO analysis for passive, moderate, and active investor archetypes
  • Multi-language Support — English, French, and Dutch output for Belgian market needs

💡 Value We Deliver

⏱️Hours of manual research reduced to seconds per query
🎯Accurate, scenario-based cost comparison for any trade size
📊Weekly automated reports delivered directly to subscribers
🔒Quality-assured by an independent LLM judge (Gemini) on every output
🌐Always up-to-date via 24-hour automated data refresh cycle
🤝Empowers sales teams to advise clients with confidence & speed

⚙️ How It Works

  • Data Ingestion: Playwright & Requests scrape 7 major Belgian broker sites daily
  • AI Extraction: GPT-4o (temp 0.0) extracts structured fee records from raw text
  • AI Structuring: Claude Sonnet validates & normalises into a canonical schema
  • Fee Engine: Computes flat, tiered, percentage, and composite fees with full auditability
  • Chat Interface: Groq / Llama 3.3 70B delivers sub-second natural language answers
  • Observability: Langfuse traces every LLM call for cost monitoring & quality scoring
